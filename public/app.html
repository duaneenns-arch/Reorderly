What is ReOrderly?
ReOrderly is a smart household reordering app. Scan any product barcode as you run out, build your reorder list automatically, and send everything to your Walmart cart with one tap. No more manually typing grocery lists or forgetting what you need.
Recipe Intelligence
Paste any recipe URL and our AI automatically extracts all ingredients with quantities. Add them to your shopping list and send to Walmart in one tap. Works with AllRecipes, Food Network, and thousands of recipe sites.
Household Sharing
Invite family members to share your list. Anyone in the household can scan items and add them — everything syncs in real time across all devices.
