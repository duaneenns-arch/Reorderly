What is an affiliate relationship?

An affiliate relationship means that when a user follows a link from ReOrderly to a retailer's website and makes a purchase, ReOrderly may receive a small commission from the retailer. This commission comes from the retailer — not from you. The price you pay is never affected by whether you arrived through an affiliate link or directly.

How ReOrderly works with Walmart

ReOrderly is a household barcode scanning app. When users scan products that are running low, ReOrderly helps match those products to listings on Walmart.com. Users can then review matching product pages and complete their purchases directly on Walmart's website.

ReOrderly has applied to participate in Walmart's affiliate program. If accepted, links to Walmart product pages generated by the app may include an affiliate tracking identifier. This allows Walmart to attribute purchases made through those links to ReOrderly and pay a commission accordingly.

What this means for you

Your shopping experience is not affected. You shop on Walmart.com directly — ReOrderly simply helps you find the right products faster. The prices you see on Walmart.com are the same whether you arrive through ReOrderly or any other means.

ReOrderly does not have access to your Walmart account, payment information, or order details. All purchases are completed on Walmart.com.
