Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: May 2026

ReOrderly may earn a commission from qualifying purchases made through links to retailer websites, if accepted into affiliate programs. This is at no additional cost to you and does not influence which products appear in ReOrderly.

1. What Is an Affiliate Program?

An affiliate program is a partnership between a website or app and a retailer. When a user clicks a link from ReOrderly to a retailer's website and makes a qualifying purchase, ReOrderly may earn a small commission from that sale. The price you pay is never affected — you pay exactly the same as if you had visited the retailer directly.

2. ReOrderly and Walmart

ReOrderly has applied to participate in the Walmart affiliate program through Impact.com. If accepted, when you tap "Review on Walmart" or click any Walmart product link in ReOrderly, you will be directed to Walmart.com. Walmart may place or recognize cookies on your browser through these links. ReOrderly may earn a commission on qualifying purchases completed on Walmart.com at no additional cost to you.

ReOrderly is an independent app and is not owned by, operated by, or officially endorsed by Walmart. Walmart is a trademark of Walmart Inc. All purchases are completed directly on Walmart.com. ReOrderly does not process payments, handle orders, or have access to your Walmart account or payment information.

3. How We Use Affiliate Links

When you tap "Review on Walmart" in the ReOrderly app or Station hub, the app opens matching Walmart product search pages for items on your household list. These links may contain an affiliate tracking parameter that identifies ReOrderly as the referring source.

ReOrderly does not:

  • Charge you any fee for using affiliate links
  • Inflate prices on linked products
  • Receive any portion of your purchase price
  • Have access to your payment information or checkout data
  • Guarantee product prices, availability, or accuracy

Always verify final product details, pricing, and availability directly on Walmart.com before purchasing.

4. Disclosure Placement

In compliance with FTC guidelines, ReOrderly displays affiliate disclosure language in the following locations:

  • App footer — visible on the main screen of the ReOrderly app
  • About section — accessible from the app menu
  • Landing page footer — visible at reorderlysolutions.com

The disclosure reads: "ReOrderly may earn a commission from qualifying purchases through retailer links if accepted into affiliate programs. This does not affect the price you pay."

ReOrderly is currently built around Walmart as the primary shopping destination. In the future, ReOrderly may participate in affiliate programs with additional retailers. This disclosure page will be updated to reflect any new affiliate relationships.
