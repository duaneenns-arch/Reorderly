How it works

Three steps.
That's it.

ReOrderly removes every bit of friction between running out of something and having it back in your home.

1
Scan as you run out
Use a Bluetooth barcode scanner or your phone camera. Scan the item as you finish it — the app instantly finds it on Walmart with the name, image, and price.
2
Your list builds itself
Items add up throughout the week. Adjust quantities, check things off, share with your whole household. Your list syncs across all devices in real time.
3
Order everything in one tap
When you're ready, tap "Order all." Your list opens store pages for every item — ready to add to your cart. Done in seconds.
