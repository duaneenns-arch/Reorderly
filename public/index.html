How it works

Three steps. That's it.

ReOrderly removes every bit of friction between running out of something and having it back in your home.

1
Scan as you run out
Use the Station's always-on barcode scanner, your phone camera, or a Bluetooth scanner. Scan the item as you finish it — ReOrderly helps match it to a Walmart product listing, with final product details, pricing, and availability confirmed on Walmart.com.
2
Your list builds itself
Items build up throughout the week on the Station and the app — in sync, in real time. Anyone in the household can add, adjust, or check items off from any device.
3
Review and order on Walmart
When you're ready, tap "Review on Walmart." ReOrderly opens matching Walmart product pages for your items — review product details, add items to your Walmart cart, and complete checkout directly on Walmart.com.
