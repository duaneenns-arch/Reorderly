How it works

Three simple steps

No learning curve. No complicated setup. Just scan, list, and order.

1
Scan items as you run low
Use a barcode scanner or your phone camera to quickly add products to your list. The app instantly finds the item on Walmart with the name, image, and price.
2
Build your reorder list automatically
ReOrderly keeps your items organized in one place, so you can review everything before you buy. Adjust quantities, check things off, or remove items anytime.
3
Order from Walmart
When you're ready, send your list to Walmart and complete checkout there. Every item opens on Walmart.com ready to add to your cart.
